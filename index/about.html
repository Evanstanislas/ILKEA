What is ILKEA?

ILKEA started as a small furniture shop in Klaten, Central Java in 2010. Despite its humble beginnings, the shop quickly gained popularity for its functional Scandinavian-inspired designs and affordable prices. Within a few years, ILKEA expanded to open its first large retail store in Jakarta in 2013. The spacious warehouse-style store allowed customers to browse a wide selection of ILKEA's minimalist and modern furniture. Also this year, we’re officially called PT. ILKEA Sinar Abadi. Over the next few years, ILKEA established itself as one of the leading furniture brands in Indonesia. It’s success lead to the first store located in Papua, opened in Merauke in 2017. ILKEA’s newest store opened just in time for the 13th anniversary of ILKEA, located in Jonggol.


Premium Products

In addition to its wide range of affordable furnishings, ILKEA also offers premium collections that showcase fine craftsmanship and luxurious materials. The Linanes leather sofa collection features hand-stitched Italian leather upholstery with a minimalist Scandinavian frame design. For the bedroom, the Rdasen collection provides an upscale look with solid acacia wood constructions and soft-close drawers.
